# Neon Visuals — Employee Experience Platform

## What We Are Building

Neon Visuals is a premium Employee Experience Platform for corporate gifting in India.

Tagline: "Where Creativity Sparks" | Domain: neonvisuals.in | HQ: Bangalore, India

We help HR managers, People Ops leaders, and founders gift employees with deeply personalised physical experiences. Every gift carries the recipient's name, a personal message, and premium packaging designed for an 8-second unboxing moment.

## Target Users

- HR Managers at funded Bangalore startups (50-500 employees)
- People Operations Leaders, Founders, CEOs, Office Admins, Culture Teams

## Core Product

- 106 SKUs across 8 product buckets (A through H): A=Welcome/Onboarding, B=Milestone/Anniversary, C=CEO/Leadership, D=Festive/Seasonal, E=Client Appreciation, F=Experience Kits, G=Tech-Forward, H=Sustainability/Eco
- Occasion-first navigation (NOT product-first)
- Gift Builder (visual kit configurator) — our killer differentiator
- Employee Memory Engine — tracks gift history per employee across years, creates switching cost moat
- EIGS with 8 archetypes: Achiever, Creator, Explorer, Builder, Root, Connector, Scholar, Minimalist
- Automated Occasion Reminders (birthdays, work anniversaries, festivals)
- Quote-based selling (NOT shopping cart e-commerce)
- Enterprise billing: GST invoices, PO, credit terms Net-15/30/60
- We are NOT a generic gifting catalog. We are the Apple of corporate gifting.
