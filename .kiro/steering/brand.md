# Neon Visuals Brand System

## Colors

- Primary Navy: #1A1A2E (trust, premium)
- Gold: #C4A35A (luxury, craft)
- Background: #FAFAF7 (warm white, NEVER cold #FFFFFF)
- Surface/Cards: #F5F0E8 (cream)
- Text Primary: #2D2D2D (charcoal)
- Text Secondary: #6B7280
- Success/Eco Green: #2D6A4F
- Wax Seal Burgundy: #7C2D36
- Border: #E5E2DC (warm gray)
- Muted: #F3F0EA

## Typography

- Headings: "Playfair Display" (Google Fonts, serif) — elegant, editorial
- Body: "DM Sans" (Google Fonts, sans-serif) — clean, modern
- Display: 48-72px, letter-spacing -0.02em
- H1: 36-48px, H2: 28-36px, H3: DM Sans semibold 20-24px
- Body: 16px, line-height 1.6

## Design Principles

- Reference: Apple.com product pages + Stripe.com storytelling + Linear.app clean aesthetic + &Open warmth
- Premium but warm (never cold corporate blue)
- Generous whitespace, intentional spacing
- Subtle animations only: fade-in, slide-up (no flashy effects)
- Warm shadows with slight gold/brown tint (not cold gray shadows)
- Cards: cream bg #F5F0E8, warm border #E5E2DC, radius 12px
- Buttons: navy bg + cream text (primary), gold border (outline), warm hover states
- All focus rings use gold #C4A35A
- Selection color: gold

## Brand Voice

- "experience kit" not "hamper" | "investment" not "cost"
- "designed for Priya" not "customisable"
- Never: "cheapest", "bulk discount", "one-stop shop"
